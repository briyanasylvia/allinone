Admissions Overview

  • New admissions are taken to the LKG based on random selection by Lottery system as per RTE(Right to Education Act). 25 % of total seats are reserved for BPL candidate.

  • False declaration about child's age is disqualification for admission and subsequent dicovery by the school authorities of such a false declaration would be grounds for dismissal.

  • Any one pressurizing the Administration through reccomendation are automatically debbared from admission.

  • Admission to other classes (other than LKG) depends on vacancies.
